Published work

SAGE
2024
Tribo-Corrosion and Mechanical Performance of Electro-deposited Nano-Composite h-BN/Epoxy Coating

Characterised electro-deposited nanocomposite hybrid coatings incorporating hBN and Epoxy in a nickel matrix for underwater anti-corrosion and anti-wear applications. Optimised via Taguchi method. Surface characterisation via XRD, FTIR, water contact angle, and crosshatch adhesion testing. Corrosion and wear resistance evaluated through electrochemical impedance spectroscopy (EIS) and pin-on-disc tribometry. Results demonstrate that incorporating hBN nanoparticles significantly enhances mechanical, tribological, and corrosion properties. Published in Proceedings of the Institution of Mechanical Engineers, Part J: Journal of Engineering Tribology.
